About:
We THE UGANDA BEEF OMASUM TRADERS ASSOCIATION(TUBOTA) are a collectivization of all and interested parties in the trade of omasum with the common goals of transforming lives of traders throughout the country through better and improved handling practices of omasum which will result in better income ,better nutrition, improved standard of living, outsourcing both domestic and foreign markets ultimately leading the economic transformation thus organize ourselves into an Association, do hereby promulgate this constitution and its by-laws

more ......



Mission:
THE UGANDA BEEF OMASUM TRADERS ASSOCIATION (TUBOTA) aims to promote, plan organize and implement development programs that will make the OMASUM traders and specifically self-sufficient and make their trade in omasum sustainable, competitive and of international standard.



Vission:

  • To assist organize and deliver an efficient package of services on a non-profit, non loss basis to all omasum traders across Uganda with the intention of maximizing th returns on their agricultural activities.


  • To establish promote and maintain programmes to enhance and improve the economic welfare of omasum traders in Uganda.


  • To provide a platform for negotiation and competitive prices for omasum traders through the country.


  • To organize cooperatives and other forms of trade organizations for omasum traders in order that they may get the full benefits of thr market and the activities of the association.


  • To promote research, development and application of science and technology to all aspects of preservation marketing and environmental impact issues, slaughtering handling, packing.


  • To promote or oppose any issue affecting the members, and to provide members with opportunities for the interchange of ideas and experiences relating to these matters.


  • To collect, co-ordinate and disseminate information and statistics of all kinds in respect to omasum trade which may be of interest or of assistance to members.


  • To represent, express and give effect to the views and opinions of members on commercial and omasum matters of all kind.


  • To enter into agreements or cooperation with the other bodies for the advancement and the protection of its members.
